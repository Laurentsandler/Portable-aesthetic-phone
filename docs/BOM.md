# Bill of Materials (BOM)

This document lists all components required to build the Portable Aesthetic Phone.

## Components

| Component | Quantity | Estimated Price | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|-------|
| Waveshare ESP32-S3 3.5" Touch LCD | 1 | ~$30 | Main development board with integrated display, touch, camera, and audio codec. [Waveshare Product Page](https://www.waveshare.com/esp32-s3-touch-lcd-3.5.htm) |
| EEMB 3.7V 2000mAh 103454 LiPo Battery | 1 | ~$15 | Search "EEMB 103454 2000mAh" on Amazon. Must have JST connector. |
| M2x8 Countersunk Screws | 4 | ~$5 | Available at hardware stores. Used to secure the case. |
| MicroSD Card | 1 | ~$10 | For storing music and photos. |

## Estimated Total Cost

**~$60** (excluding 3D printing materials)

## 3D Printed Parts

The following parts need to be 3D printed (STL files located in the `CAD` folder):

| Part | File | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Main Case Body | `Case.stl` | Dark blue PLA recommended |
| Button | `Button.stl` | Same material as case |

### Recommended Print Settings

- **Material:** PLA (dark blue recommended)
- **Supports:** Not required
- **Infill:** 20% or higher

## Where to Buy

- **Waveshare ESP32-S3 Board:** [Waveshare Official](https://www.waveshare.com/esp32-s3-touch-lcd-3.5.htm) or Amazon
- **Battery:** Amazon (search "EEMB 103454 2000mAh")
- **Screws:** Local hardware store or Amazon
- **MicroSD Card:** Any electronics retailer
